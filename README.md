# Warm-Connect

## Problem to solve
To identify early signs of loneliness and give personalised reconnection strategies to help improve mental health and well-being.

## Context
With increasing reliance on social media, many people don't realise they lack real-life social connections. Human beings were designed to be social creatures, finding comfort and warmth in good company. In today's world, plenty of us suffer from loneliness without realising it. Prolonged loneliness is one of the major causes of mental health issues like anxiety and sometimes even depression.
Our solution
A social well-being and behaviour analytics app that identifies early signs of loneliness and gives personalised reconnection strategies.

## Global Need
According to the latest research, about 1 in 6 people suffer from loneliness, and its impact on mental health is quite significant. Anyone struggling with mental health issues can check whether one of the root causes is loneliness. This is a good initial step to understand why mental well-being is being impacted.

Sometimes, one may even be aware that they are lonely but fail to fathom why or how to get out of it.

## Benefits of 'WarmConnect':
The app can help users to:
Understand their type of loneliness
Give a metric of how lonely they might be, based on survey questions.
Provide personalised suggestions and possible solutions to help overcome loneliness.
We know it can be challenging to take action in such scenarios, which is why the app gives:
Small quantifiable tasks
Fun mini-challenges
Daily connection goals
Tailored strategies to reconnect with friends
Strategies to make new friends
It can also generate conversation starters for users to connect with people via text and initiate social meetups.
